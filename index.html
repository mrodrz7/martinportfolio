I've been coding since 2019 but I first grew interest while pursuing a career in Quality Assurance in 2018 at Year Up Greater Boston. One of my assignments consisted of creating a static blog using just HTML & CSS - it was there that I realized I wanted to be on the creative side of Software Development and I started learning by using Codecademy and W3 Schools. Eventually I took a risk and attended General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive and shortly after landed my first contract role as a Front End Engineer! Although, I am not currently employed as a dev, my services are available for freelance opportunities!
